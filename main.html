Summary

Inception is a science fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. The movie follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals information by infiltrating dreams. Given a chance to have his criminal history erased, he must do the impossible: **inception**—planting an idea in someone's mind instead of stealing it.

Main Actors
